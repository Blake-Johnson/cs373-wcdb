About the World Crisis Database

WCDb Info

There cannot be a crisis next week. My schedule is already full.

Henry Kissinger in The New York Times Magazine

In the bustling world of the 21st century, there's hardly time for personal crises - let alone global ones. WCDb aims to amend that issue by giving you the power to control your life, allowing you to choose when to care about the rest of humanity and when to enjoy the more personal aspects of life.

Drought in Austrailia? But your swim meet is in 15 minutes. Hungry children in Africa? Unfortunately, you must attend an important dinner party with your coworkers. Afterwards, when the party (and after-party) is over and dust has settled, relive all the events suffered by people around the world - without the suffering, of course - on the WCDb website. Always here. Always ready when you are.
